Shipping Code vs. Understanding AI

2 min read
Shashank Raj
#ai #engineering

Developer value has fundamentally shifted in the post-LLM era. Rather than raw coding speed, the critical skill is now comprehending what gets shipped.

Two Paths

When working with AI tools, every developer is implicitly choosing between two modes:

Path A — Ship and Forget: Use AI solely for output velocity. Deliver minimum viable products quickly. Maximum throughput, minimum cognitive load.

Path B — Ship and Learn: Leverage AI interactively to deepen knowledge and validate assumptions while building. Use it as a persistent learning resource available 24/7. Build capacity for future improvements, not just the current task.

The Differentiator

Path B is the differentiator. The most valuable developers won't be the fastest — they'll be the most informed.

When an AI generates something, the developer who asks "why does this work?" and traces the answer is accumulating a compounding advantage over the developer who just ships and moves on. The gap widens with every task.

AI as an accelerator is table stakes. AI as a teacher is the edge.

Are your teams currently prioritising speed over understanding?
